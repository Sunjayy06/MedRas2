How researchers use MedRAS

An eight-step research lifecycle

  1. Define the research objective.
  2. Design the study with the AI-assisted builder.
  3. Calculate the required sample size.
  4. Generate the proposal in your institution's format.
  5. Upload data and run statistical analysis.
  6. Compile structured results.
  7. Draft the thesis or manuscript.
  8. Verify originality and citation compliance.

About MedRAS

Not a chat bot. A research operating system.

MedRAS is purpose-built for medical, biomedical, and academic researchers who need methodological rigour at every step. The interface is structured and menu-driven; statistical computation is performed by validated libraries, not by language models. AI assists with planning and writing — never with numerical results.
