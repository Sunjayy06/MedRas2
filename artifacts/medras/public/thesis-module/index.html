How the upper hand is enforced

Researcher always in control

  • Every AI suggestion appears as an inline track-change with a ✓ / ✗ — nothing auto-applies.
  • Every number from your statistical output is locked in yellow and cannot be rewritten.
  • AI never writes a sentence without an attached source from the retrieved papers.
  • Chapter dashboard with traffic-light progress; jump in/out in any order.
  • Helper buttons take you to the right MedRAS module (Sample Size, Stats, Plagiarism) when you need them.
