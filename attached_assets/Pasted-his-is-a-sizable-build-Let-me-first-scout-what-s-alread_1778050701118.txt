Consent translation flow

Hard-coded 8-section English template filled with the user's metadata.
For each selected secondary language: best-effort Gemini 2.5 Flash translation with a hardened prompt (treats source as data, requires Western digits, preserves underscore signature lines and proper names in Latin script, no markdown).
Post-translation validator rejects output that doesn't have all 8 numbered headings or at least 3 underscore signature lines — protects against malformed consent reaching an Ethics Committee.
Stable cache key (md5 of template) survives process restarts.
Translation failure or rejection ⇒ a clearly-labelled placeholder page asking for a manually-reviewed translation.
