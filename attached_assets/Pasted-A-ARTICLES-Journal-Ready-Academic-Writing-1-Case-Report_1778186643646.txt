A. ARTICLES (Journal-Ready Academic Writing)
1. Case Report – ₹6,000
Deliverables:
• 6,000-word structured case report (CARE guidelines)
• 10–15 citations (Vancouver)
• Plagiarism <10%, AI-text <10%
Delivery Time: 3–4 days
2. Case Series – ₹8,000
Deliverables:
• 8,000-word detailed case series (STROBE guidelines)
• Tables, figures, clinical summary charts
• 15–20 citations
• Plag <10%, AI <10%
Delivery Time: 4–5 days
3. Narrative Review – ₹8,000
Deliverables:
• 8,000-word narrative review with emerging public-health perspectives
• 20–25 citations
• Clear thematic sections + conceptual model
• Plag <10%, AI <10%
Delivery Time: 5–6 days
4. Systematic Review – ₹15,000
Deliverables:
• 12,000–15,000-word PRISMA-compliant systematic review
• Search strategy, PRISMA flowchart, risk-of-bias assessment
• 40–60 citations
• Plag <10%, AI <10%
Delivery Time: 7–10 days
5. Meta-analysis – ₹15,000
Deliverables:
• 12,000–15,000-word meta-analysis (PRISMA + MOOSE)
• Forest plots, funnel plots, heterogeneity stats (I²)
• 40–70 citations
• Plag <10%, AI <10%
Delivery Time: 10–12 days
6. Original Research Article – ₹15,000
Types Covered:
Cross-sectional, case–control, cohort, RCT, quasi-experimental, mixed-methods, KAP studies, qualitative interviews/FGDs, community-based surveys, hospital-based observational designs.
Deliverables:
• 12,000–15,000-word manuscript (STROBE/CONSORT/COREQ as relevant)
• Tables, graphs, statistical output
• 25–40 citations
• Plag <10%, AI <10%
Delivery Time: 7–10 days
B. Postgraduate Thesis Support – ₹40,000
Deliverables:
• Full PG thesis (80–100 pages)
• Standard design based on need (cross-sectional/cohort/case-control/qualitative/mixed methods)
• Sample size calculation + complete statistics
• 50–60 references (Vancouver)
• Plag <10%, AI text <15%
Delivery Time: 20–25 days
C. Monograph Writing – ₹5,000 to 8000
Deliverables:
• Complete monograph (70–80 pages)
• Topic & design customized to requirement
• Full data interpretation, illustrations, tables
• 40–50 citations
• Plag <10%, AI text <15%
Delivery Time: 15–20 days
